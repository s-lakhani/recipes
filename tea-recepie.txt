Things you would need:

- Tea Dust
- Drinking Water
- Sugar
- Milk

Steps:

- Boil half cup of water
- Add 1 tea spoon of tea dust
- Add sugar to taste
- Let it boil for a 5 or so mins and then add milk
- Now the tea will indicate you that its done, if you don't turn off the stove the tea will come out :)
